I am a postdoctoral researcher working on agroecosystem and forest ecosystem modelling,
with a focus on ecosystem responses to disturbances, resilience, and adaptive management.

My research combines process-based modelling and decision-support approaches to
analyse how managed ecosystems respond to environmental and management pressures.

using conceptual and quantitative models to assess multifunctionality and long-term
sustainability under disturbance regimes. In parallel, I have developed and evaluated
